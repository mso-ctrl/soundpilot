Philosophy

AI does the analysis.
You make the calls.

SoundPilot is designed around three roles. AI handles pattern recognition and analysis. Artists keep creative control and final decisions. Humans — marketers, curators, collaborators — provide expertise the AI can't replicate.

The platform doesn't replace the music industry. It removes the barriers that kept independent artists out of it.
